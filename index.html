I am Richard Ruggerio, Design Principal & Executive Creative Director with IBM Consulting (IBM iX). Over my 25+ year career I have worked across a remarkable range of industries and brands—always driven by the same restless question my grandfather taught me to ask: why?

Growing up, my grandmother was a poet and my grandfather a furniture maker and painter. Their curiosity was infectious. My grandfather handed me old appliances and TVs to dismantle, then lego blocks to recombine—instilling in me a design philosophy I still carry: always dig deeper, tear things down, rebuild them to form a meaningful connection to others.

I joined IBM through an acquisition and have since become a member of the iX Global Creative Council, co-lead of the Design System Guild, and a named IBM Design Principal. I am proudest of the people I have had the privilege to lead and mentor—the strategists, UX/UI designers, researchers, and developers who make the work worth doing.
